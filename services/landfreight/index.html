Land Freight

Land Freight Quote

Leverage on our local knowledge of the lands and places of West Africa. Only few things in life give as much confidence!




We focus on both domestic and international movement of goods by land through cost-effective, reliable and on-time delivery solutions. By road or rail, we can take your shipments to/from anywhere in West Africa, in the right direction, with deliver as per commitment - because we work to understand your objectives and create solutions that deliver.

All aspects of the transportation process including information exchange, management and operations are effectively and efficiently linked to offer our customers total flexibility and transparency.

  • Full-truck-loads (FTL) and Less-than-truck-loads (LTL) services
  • Specialized equipment - Low bed, Extra Wide & Self-Geared Trailers.
  • Right capacity allocation and equipment for every type of shipment
  • Complete infrastructure at all border points - inter-country and inter-state
  • Temperature-controlled Transportation
